The Leisure Sports Journey

Royal Gaming (PVT) LTD, operating under the brand "Leisure Sports," launched its operations in 2019 by acquiring a significant portion of the business from one of the only two live Sportsbook operators in Sri Lanka.

With a vision of becoming the best Sportsbook Operator in the Asian Sub-Continent, Leisure Sports aims to establish itself as the most customer-friendly operator in Sri Lanka within a short span of time.

Since inception, we have embraced a customer-first approach through initiatives like the "A Winner from a Loser - Cash Back Raffle Draw," "Winner to Win a Beer," and regular "Customer Meet Ups."

We have cultivated a distinctive flavor and culture designed to challenge the traditional bookie—bringing you into a space filled with true excitement and joy to follow your favorite sport.
