The Journey of
Leisure Sports

Royal Gaming (PVT) LTD, operating under the renowned brand "Leisure Sports," launched its operations in 2019 by acquiring a significant portion of the business from one of the only two live Sportsbook operators in Sri Lanka.

Since our inception, we have fully embraced a customer-first philosophy. Through exciting community initiatives like the "A Winner from a Loser - Cash Back Raffle Draw", "Winner to Win a Beer", and our regular "Customer Meet Ups", Leisure Sports has rapidly gained immense acceptance among the majority of elite punters.

We have cultivated a distinctive flavor and culture deliberately designed to challenge the traditional bookie format—bringing you into a sophisticated space filled with true excitement and joy to follow your favorite sport.

Our Mission

To deliver an unparalleled, premium sports entertainment experience. We are committed to offering competitive odds, comprehensive global market coverage, and a highly secure, responsible gaming environment that prioritizes customer satisfaction above all else.

Our Vision

To establish ourselves as the best and most customer-friendly Sportsbook Operator in Sri Lanka and across the Asian Sub-Continent. We aim to revolutionize the betting industry by merging traditional excitement with modern, transparent, and rewarding community practices.
